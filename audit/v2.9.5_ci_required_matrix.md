## Why these six

### benchmark integrated smoke

Purpose:

- protect the frozen same-config regression baseline
- confirm benchmark remains replay-auditable under integrated execution

What it is allowed to assert:

- required artifacts exist
- manifest / inventory / replay audit are complete
- `comparison_type == "same-config"`

What it must not assert:

- cross-regime numerical comparison against smoke or production

### production integrated smoke

Purpose:

- protect the `cross-regime` operational guard
- confirm production cannot be reinterpreted as a benchmark same-config baseline

What it is allowed to assert:

- `comparison_type == "cross-regime"` across manifest, reports, and replay audit
- `same-config` override rejection

What it must not assert:

- benchmark vs production score or reason-distribution comparison

### CI-sized full fixture

Purpose:

- keep `full` mode continuously executable in CI
- cover Cap + assays + Layer2 + inventory/replay wiring with a small deterministic fixture

Fixture envelope:

- library rows: `2`
- caps rows: `2`
- assay rows: `2`

### config guard matrix

Purpose:

- keep taxonomy, comparison-type, and regression-baseline rules closed

### replay audit / inventory cross-check

Purpose:

- keep completion semantics and replay consistency machine-checkable

### Cap artifact invariants

Purpose:

- stop row-unit and truth-source regressions before they leak into integrated reports

## Artifact stability judgement

The promoted set is based on deterministic or tightly controlled fixtures:

- benchmark smoke: real-data library subset, stubbed core bridge, managed theta table, PAT diagnostics
- production smoke: same as benchmark smoke, but guarded as `cross-regime`
- full fixture: fully synthetic small fixture

The CI signal is therefore centered on:

- artifact existence
- contract semantics
- replay readiness
- taxonomy guards

It is not centered on raw score-distribution equality across different operating regimes.

belongs to `V-1`. If branch protection policy wants a dry-run window first, the same workflow
can be enabled before the checks are marked required in repository settings.
